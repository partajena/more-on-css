Google Fonts is a library of free and open-source font families, providing an easy way to add a wide variety of fonts to your web projects. It allows you to embed custom fonts on your website and use them just like standard web fonts.

The library includes a diverse range of fonts, from classic to modern, ensuring that you can find the perfect typography for your website. With Google Fonts, you can enhance the visual appeal of your site and create a unique and engaging user experience.
